Clawson, Michigan

Keeping up with your town shouldn't be this hard.

People in Clawson care about the place they live. The hard part is keeping up with it. What is happening is spread across meeting portals, social feeds, flyers, and PDFs, and by the time you find the thing you cared about, the moment to weigh in or show up has often passed.

Commonwealth is one clear, plain-language place to see what is happening, follow the few things you care about most, and know how to take part while there is still time.

Why Clawson

Clawson is the little city with a big heart, a place where people show up for each other. That is why we are starting here. Commonwealth covers the whole of the town's life: what the city is deciding, what is happening at the library, the schools, and the senior center, the town's history, and the local groups doing steady, quiet work. The things that make a town a town, easier to find and easier to be part of.

For the people who keep it going

So much of a town runs on a few stretched people: staff, volunteers, coaches, organizers. They already do the work. Commonwealth exists to help more of the town find it, with each group posting in its own name, for free, and nothing new to learn or maintain. As we build, we are sitting down with Clawson's institutions one at a time, so this fits the way they actually work.

Be the first to know when it opens.

Leave your email and we will tell you when Commonwealth opens in Clawson. That is the only thing we will use it for.

No tracking, no ads, no selling your address. One message when it opens, and only the weekly update after that if you want it. Leave anytime.

A note on what this is. Commonwealth is an independent, nonprofit community project, free for residents and built to run lean. We are in the early stages of setting up the nonprofit that will support this work for the long run. Clawson is first, and Clawson is home. In time, we hope what starts here can help other small towns with big hearts.
